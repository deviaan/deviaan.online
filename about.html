Hi there! My name is Juan, known online as deviaan. I made this site as a hobby, something fun I can do that gives me somewhere to share whatever I'm currently interested in.

The whole point of this site is to be personal--to me that means small, easy to maintain, and, most importantly, a reflection of who I am. I want to try and make everything on the website myself. Especially all the things that I don't know how to do well or at all. While this does mean that I probably won't put anything there of any real value, but it might still be useful to someone, at least as a distraction.

Anyways, I hope you get as much of a kick out of the stuff you find here as I had in putting it together!
